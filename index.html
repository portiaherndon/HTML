Golden Era Entertainment

Golden Era Entertainment, founded and established by Portia "P-Dub" Herndon in 2016, is a music label that houses many artists, producers, engineers, and songwriters. Some of those artists include Jay-Z, Kanye West, Big Sean, 2Chainz, Omarion, DJ Khaled, and many others.

P-Dub

Portia "P-Dub" Herndon was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania in the year of 1992. Having attended catholic from kindergarten to all throughout high school, she decided to leave the big city and attend college at Howard University. That is where she met entrepreneur, Sean "Diddy" Combs. P-Dub and Diddy hit it off instantly; upon graduation in May of 2014, P-Dub became a full-time employee at Bad Boy Entertainment as a producer, where she learned the ins-and-outs of the music industry. Two years later is when she decided to start her own label, Golden Era Entertainment and the rest is history!
